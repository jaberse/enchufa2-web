# EV Factory Location & Battery Assembly Research
## Batch 2 - Asian + Other Brands (2025-2026 European/Spanish Market)

**Research Date:** 2026-04-08
**Purpose:** Verify factory locations and battery assembly for Plan Auto+ "tramo europeo" subsidy eligibility in Spain

---

## Key Findings Summary

### Plan Auto+ "Tramo Europeo" Requirements
For maximum Spanish subsidy eligibility, vehicles must meet the "EEE" criterion:
- **E**lectric: 100% pure electric
- **E**conomic: Price < €35,000 (excl. tax)
- **E**uropean: BOTH final vehicle assembly AND battery assembly in EU

Vehicles with NO EU battery assembly receive significantly reduced subsidy levels (~50% of maximum).

## Critical Observations

### Fully EU-Eligible (Plan Auto+ "Tramo Europeo" - Maximum Subsidy):
1. **Hyundai Kona Electric 65 kWh** - Czech + Polish supply chain
2. **Mercedes-Benz EQA 250+** - German factory + European batteries
3. **Mercedes-Benz EQB 250+** - Hungarian factory + EU battery systems
4. **Tesla Model Y AWD Long Range** - German (Berlin) factory + mixed EU/overseas batteries

### Conditionally Eligible (2026 onwards after manufacturing launches):
1. **BYD models (Atto 3, Dolphin, Seal)** - Hungarian assembly from Q2 2026 IF battery assembly confirmed in Hungary
2. **BYD Seal U** - Turkey production does NOT count (Turkey ≠ EU member)
3. **BYD Dolphin Surf** - Q2 2026 Hungary start with announced battery assembly

### NOT Eligible (Imports from Non-EU):
- All Hyundai models except Kona: Korea sourced
- All Kia models: Korea sourced
- All MG models: China sourced
- All Nissan models: Japan sourced
- All Smart models: China sourced
- Tesla Model 3 variants: China sourced
- Tesla Model Y RWD variants: China sourced
- Toyota bZ4X: Japan sourced

### Special Cases:
- **Tesla Model Y**: SPLIT production - Shanghai supplies most of EU market (NOT eligible), but Berlin/Grünheide production for some AWD variants IS eligible
- **BYD models**: Currently ALL imports from China, but massive manufacturing investment in Hungary and Turkey (Turkey ≠ EU) will change eligibility from 2026
