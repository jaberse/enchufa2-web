## Detailed Model Notes

### VW GROUP (MEB Platform)
- **Battery Assembly Strategy**: Centralized at Zwickau (Germany), Emden (Germany), Mlada Boleslav (Czech Republic), with suppliers including VW PowerCo (Salzgitter), CATL Hungary, and Skoda's new facility
- **EU Qualification**: All MEB models manufactured in EU with EU battery assembly = HIGH Plan Auto+ eligibility

### RENAULT GROUP (Douai/Ampere)
- **Battery Partners**: AESC (on-site), CATL Hungary, LG Chem Poland
- **Assembly**: Douai gigafactory (Ampere ElectriCity) handles battery module assembly
- **EU Qualification**: All Douai models = QUALIFIED for Plan Auto+

### STELLANTIS (Pan-European)
- **Battery Strategy**: CATL Hungary for LFP, developing Zaragoza JV with CATL (target 2026)
- **Current Status**: Citroën ë-C3 qualified; Opel Corsa/Mokka qualified via Hungary routing
- **Note**: Fiat 500e Turin assembly likely EU-sourced but exact supplier needs verification

### BMW GROUP
- **Battery Assembly**: Leipzig (Germany) produces battery modules for i4/iX/i5
- **Qualification**: All current models = QUALIFIED for Plan Auto+

## Key Findings

### Plan Auto+ "Tramo Europeo" Eligibility Criteria
Based on Spanish government electrification incentives:
1. **Factory Assembly**: Final assembly must be in EU
2. **Battery Assembly**: Battery pack assembly must be in EU (not just cells)
3. **Critical Detail**: Cell sourcing origin less critical than PACK assembly location

### HIGH Eligibility (Confirmed EU+EU)
- Audi Q4, Q6 e-tron
- BMW i4, i5, iX1, iX2
- Cupra Born
- Ford Explorer, Puma Gen-E
- Opel Corsa Electric, Mokka-e
- Peugeot e-208, e-2008
- Renault 5, Megane, Scenic E-Tech
- Škoda Elroq, Enyaq
- VW ID.3, ID.4, ID.7, ID.Buzz
- Volvo EX30, EX40
- Jeep Avenger Electric

### MEDIUM Eligibility (Needs Verification)
- Citroën ë-C3 (Trnava/Kragujevac factory + CATL Hungary battery = likely YES)
- Fiat 500e (Turin factory needs battery sourcing confirmation)

### NOT Eligible (Non-EU Factory)
- Cupra Tavascan (China)
- Dacia Spring (China)
- Mini Aceman, Mini Cooper SE (China)
- Polestar 2, Polestar 4 (China/non-EU)
